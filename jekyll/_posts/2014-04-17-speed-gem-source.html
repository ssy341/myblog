--- layout: article_bash title: 更换ruby源，解决gem install可能安装速度慢或者失败 date: 2014-4-17 categories: [jekyll] tags: [jekyll,gem sources] ---

首先移除默认源 

$ gem sources --remove https://rubygems.org/

再添加淘宝ruby源 

gem sources -a http://ruby.taobao.org/

然后检查是否添加成功 

gem sources -l
*** CURRENT SOURCES ***

http://ruby.taobao.org # 请确保只有 ruby.taobao.org

参考：http://ruby.taobao.org/
